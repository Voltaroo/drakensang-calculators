Why was critical damage removed from calculator?

Version v1.2.0 of critical chance calculator removed critical hit from calculator due to update of the DSO files, since critical hit is same for all difficulties (despite having critical hit data for all of the level 1 - 145). I apologize for misleading and want to thank discord user Naincarnation for highlighting this problem.
